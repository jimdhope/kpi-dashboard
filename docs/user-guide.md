# User and Agent Guide

Welcome to the **KPI Quest User Guide**! Whether you are an **Agent** working hard to reach your targets, a **Team Leader** supporting your pod, or a general user seeking documentation or tools, this guide will walk you through everything you need to know about navigating the application.

---

## 🚪 Logging In & Role-Based Dashboards

KPI Quest features **automatic role-based redirects**. When you log in, the system determines your role and presents you with a dashboard custom-tailored to your daily activities:

* **Agents:** Landing automatically on the `/agent` dashboard. Focuses on personal targets, today's accomplishments, current active competitions, gamification stats (badges, points, levels), and personal performance charts.
* **Team Leaders / Pod Managers:** Automatically routed to `/team-leader` or `/pod-manager` respectively. Focuses on pod comparisons, tracking team performance, review logs, and motivating team members.
* **Competition Runners:** Routed to `/competition-runner` to manage ongoing scoreboards.
* **Campaign Managers:** Directed to `/campaign-manager` to configure rules and review logs.
* **Administrators:** Routed to `/admin` to control configuration settings, data, and system integrity.

*Note: Your primary email and temporary password will be provided by your Administrator. On your first login, you will be prompted to choose a secure, custom password.*

## 🏆 Competitions & Live Standings

Competitions are gamified challenges running over a set period (e.g., Daily, Weekly, or Monthly).

### Key Features on the Competitions Dashboard:
1. **Live Leaderboards:** View real-time standings of agents and teams participating in active competitions.
2. **Personal Progress vs. Team Standings:**
   - **Today's Score:** Displayed in the central table to show immediate focus on what you have accomplished today.
   - **Cumulative Total:** Displayed in separate standings lists to show the overall competition metrics from start to finish.
3. **Emoji Key Legend:** Each competition rule has an associated emoji (e.g., 📞 for Calls, 🌟 for Exceptional Quality, 📝 for Documentations). Check the Emoji Key Legend on your competition dashboard to see which rule matches which emoji.
4. **Log Daily Accomplishments:**
   - Go to **Competitions → Log** or click the "Log Scores" button on your dashboard.
   - Select your current competition and input your results (e.g., rule checkmarks or counts).
   - Click "Submit Scores" to update the live leaderboards instantly!
5. **Certificates & Badges:**
   - Under **Competitions → Certificates**, top-performing agents can generate and print beautiful, personalized certificates to celebrate high achievement.
   - Visit your **Profile** to view the collection of unique Badges you have earned!

## 📊 Performance Tracking & Daily Trackers

Trackers are used for ongoing, campaign-wide KPI analysis separate from short-term competitions.

### Daily Trackers
* Navigate to **Trackers** in your sidebar.
* Select the relevant Tracker KPI (e.g., "Inbound Support Tickets") and record your value.
* This updates your ongoing metrics and assists your Team Leader in tracking performance averages.

### Performance Charts
* Go to the **Performance** tab to view your performance overview.
* View interactive charts mapping out your logs over a daily, weekly, or monthly timeline.
* Compare your actual scores against your targets to identify areas of strength or opportunities for coaching.

## 📖 Knowledge Base

The Knowledge Base (KB) is your go-to digital library for guidelines, policy sheets, procedures, and instructional articles.

### Browsing Articles
* Go to the **Knowledge Base** tab to view a modern, clean single-column browse view.
* Click on **Categories** or search using tags to narrow down what you are looking for.
* Expand parent and subcategories easily from the left-hand navigation.

### Creating & Editing Articles
*(Availability depends on user role privileges)*
* Click **Create Article** or edit an existing draft.
* Use our feature-rich **Tiptap Rich Text Editor** to style your article with bullet lists, headers, images, code-blocks, text styling, and table grids.
* Set the status to **Published** to make it visible to the team, or **Draft** if you are still composing.

---

## 📇 Shared Directory

The Directory is a centralized address book for contacts within and outside the organization.

### Finding a Contact
* Go to the **Directory** tab.
* Filter contacts by **Type** (Internal department, Partner, Vendor, External contact), **Company**, or **Department**.
* Use the search bar to locate specific individuals, email addresses, or phone numbers.

### Adding Contacts
* Users with permissions can click the "Add Contact" button to launch the creation modal.
* Assign appropriate company names, departments, and enter comma-separated tags (e.g., `Sales, Escalation, Out-of-Hours`) to categorize the entry.

## 🛠️ Productivity Tools & Mini-Games

KPI Quest hosts a collection of practical tools to make your daily tasks simpler:

### Utility Calculators
Navigate to **Tools** in the top navigation to find:
- **Instalment Plan Calculator:** Quickly determine monthly or weekly payment structures for customers setting up repayment terms.
- **Energy Usage & Tariff Comparison:** Compare different utilities or energy tariffs based on standing charges, unit rates, and estimated annual consumptions.
- **Dual Fuel Calculator:** Consolidate gas and electricity figures to verify dual-fuel discounts.
- **Meter Reading Guide:** Read step-by-step instructions on assisting customers with submitting manual meter reads for credit/debit balances.

### Mini-Games: Rock, Paper, Scissors (RPS)
Take a quick mental break or engage in a friendly duel:
- Go to **Mini-Games → Rock Paper Scissors**.
- Throw Rock, Paper, or Scissors against the computerized opponent.
- Keep track of your score, win streaks, and earn points for your profile!
