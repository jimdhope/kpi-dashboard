# Competition Runner Guide - KPI Quest 2

This guide is for **Competition Runners**. It covers how to design, create, and manage competitions for your team.

## 🏆 Competition Lifecycle

Competitions go through three phases: **Creation**, **Active**, and **Review**.

---

## 🎨 Designing a Competition

Before creating a competition, consider:
- **Duration**: How long will it run? (e.g., 1 week, 1 month)
- **Goal**: What are you trying to achieve? (e.g., increase sales, improve resolution rate)
- **Rules**: How will points be earned?
- **Prizes**: What will the winners receive?

---

## ➕ Creating a Competition

1.  Navigate to the **Competitions** tab.
2.  Click **Create Competition**.
3.  Provide a **Name** and a **Description** for the competition.
4.  Set the **Start Date** and **End Date**.
5.  Choose the **Participating Pods**.
6.  Define the **Points Rules** based on campaign KPIs.
7.  Click **Launch Competition**.

---

## 📊 Managing Active Competitions

Monitor the progress of active competitions to ensure everything is running smoothly.

### 🥇 Monitoring the Leaderboard
1.  Navigate to **Competitions > Dashboard**.
2.  View the real-time ranking of **Individual Agents**, **Teams**, and **Pods**.
3.  Identify top performers and share their achievements with the team.

---

## 🏁 Reviewing Completed Competitions

Once a competition has ended, review the results and celebrate the winners.

1.  View the **Final Standings** in the competition dashboard.
2.  Analyze the **Performance Data** to see if the competition's goals were achieved.
3.  Export the results for performance reports.
4.  Announce and reward the winners!

---

## 🤝 Need Support?

If you have questions about competition rules or technical issues, please contact your **Admin** or **Campaign Manager**.
