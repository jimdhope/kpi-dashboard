# Team Leader Guide - KPI Quest 2

This guide is for **Team Leaders**. It covers your specific responsibilities for managing team performance and engagement.

## 👥 Team Management

As a Team Leader, you are responsible for monitoring your team's daily progress.

### 📊 Monitoring Team Performance
1.  Navigate to your **Dashboard**.
2.  Review your team's aggregate scores on the **Leaderboard**.
3.  Use the **Performance** tab to analyze individual agent trends within your team.
4.  Identify top performers and areas where coaching might be needed.

### 📝 Reviewing Achievement Logs
1.  Navigate to **Trackers > Log**.
2.  Filter the logs by your team members to verify their entries.
3.  (Optional) Provide feedback on achievements or clarify KPI rules with your agents.

---

## 🏆 Participating in Competitions

Support your team during competitions and ensure everyone is engaged.

1.  Monitor the **Live Competition Leaderboard**.
2.  Use your team's communication channel to share motivation and updates.
3.  Ensure all team members are logging their achievements daily to maximize the team's total score.

---

## 🤝 Collaboration

Collaborate with Pod Managers and Campaign Managers to ensure your team's goals align with the overall campaign objectives.

-   **Pod Alignment**: Ensure your team is contributing effectively to the pod's overall targets.
-   **KPI Clarification**: If there is confusion about how a specific KPI should be logged, consult with the Campaign Manager.
