# Agent Guide - KPI Quest 2

This guide is for **Agents**. It covers how to use your personal dashboard and participate in team competitions.

## 🚀 My Dashboard (`/agent`)

Your personal hub for tracking daily progress and seeing where you stand.
- **Competitions Card**: Quick view of active competition standings (Team and Agent leaderboards).
- **Trackers Card**: Summary of your rolling tracker counts for the day.
- **Performance Card**: 6-week KPI breakdown table showing your recent trends.
- **Mini Games Card**: Quick link to current games and the all-time Top 10 leaderboard.

## 🏆 Competitions (`/agent/competitions`)

Track your contribution to the team's success.
- **Team Leaderboard**: See the ranking of all teams in your pod.
- **Your Score Breakdown**: A detailed view of every point you've earned, categorized by KPI rules (with emojis!).
- **Agent Standings**: See how you rank individually against other agents in the competition.

## 🎯 Trackers (`/agent/trackers`)

Monitor your "Rolling Trackers" - these are daily targets separate from competitions.
- **Your Rolling Trackers**: Visual cards showing your total count for every tracker KPI today.
- **Tracker Leaderboard**: A daily ranking of all agents by their combined tracker scores.
- **Date Filtering**: Use the calendar to review your tracker history from previous days.

## 📈 Performance Trends (`/agent/performance`)

Analyze your long-term consistency over the last 6 weeks.
- **Weekly Breakdown**: A comprehensive table showing your total score for every KPI per week.
- **Performance Trend Chart**: A visual line graph of your performance for a selected KPI.
- **Targets & Benchmarks**: If your pod has targets set, they will appear as reference lines on your charts.

## 🎮 Mini Games (`/agent/mini-games`)

Foster team spirit with quick competitive activities.
- **Rock Paper Scissors** (`/agent/rps`): Challenge teammates and track your wins.
- **Game Standings**: View the all-time leaderboard to see the top-performing players.

## 👤 Profile & Settings (`/agent/profile`)

- **Profile Information**: Update your Display Name and see your current Pod assignment.
- **Security**: Change your password at any time.
- **Notifications**: (Coming Soon) Manage how you receive updates about achievements and competition milestones.
